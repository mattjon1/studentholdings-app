Who am I?

I am currently a freshman at Northwestern University, pursuing my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. I have working proficiency in Java and Python and am currently advancing my skills as a front-end web designer. I was born on July 21, 1999, and was raised in Niagara Falls, Canada for the better part of my life, but I spent my high school career in Irvine, California. I have been playing hockey since I was three years old, and my favorite team is the Buffalo Sabres. I am a strong advocate for mental health education and the destigamtization of mental health discussion. I have served as the President of the Student Mental Illness Learning Effort and have worked Directly with the National Alliance on Mental Illness in order to bring better mental health resources to my district. My future goals are to utilize my accomplishments and skills as a student in order to fulfill my goal as a human being: philanthropy, whether that is through mental health or otherwise.
